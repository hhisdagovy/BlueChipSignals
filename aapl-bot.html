Best Practices for Success

Track Product Cycles & Launch Events

AAPL's stock price is heavily influenced by anticipation and reaction to its product announcements (especially iPhones). Be aware of the dates of these events.

Monitor Major Supplier News

News from key suppliers (like TSMC or Foxconn) can provide early clues about production levels and potential supply chain issues, impacting AAPL's stock.

AAPL as a Market Bellwether

Due to its massive market cap, AAPL's performance can influence the entire market. If AAPL is strong, it often provides a tailwind for the SPY and QQQ.

Watch for Orderly Pullbacks

AAPL often trends in a very structured way. Our bot looks for signals during pullbacks to key support levels within an established uptrend, offering high-probability entries.

Pay Attention to Options Volume

Large, unusual options activity can foreshadow a significant move. Look for high call or put volume as confirmation of the direction indicated by our signal.
