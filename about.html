Our Story

Built by traders,
for traders.

Blue Chip Signals was born out of frustration with noisy, unreliable trading advice. We spent years developing and backtesting a strategy that actually works — then built the tools to make it accessible to everyone.

Years of
relentless work.

What began as a personal project to improve trading results grew into a full platform built from the ground up. Here's how it happened.

2020

Strategy Development Begins

It all began with testing a momentum breakout strategy across SPY with hundreds of hours of data analysis to try and find an edge.

2022

First Private Telegram Groups

A small private group of traders tests the signals in live market conditions. The results validate years of testing and development work.

2025

Blue Chip Signals Goes Public

The platform officially launches, expanding coverage to META, NVDA, AAPL, TSLA and AMZN — bringing institutional-grade signals to everyday traders.

2026

Platform Expansion

The Trading Journal and Trade Planner ship — giving members the tools to track performance, plan entries, and grow as disciplined traders.
