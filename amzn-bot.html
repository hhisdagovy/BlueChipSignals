Best Practices for Success

Follow Consumer & Cloud News

AMZN is driven by its retail and AWS cloud segments. Watch for economic data on consumer spending and news about cloud computing contracts and competition.

Patience with Setups

Compared to more volatile stocks, AMZN can take longer to develop its intraday trends. The best signals often come after a period of consolidation. Don't rush an entry.

Respect Longer-Term Moving Averages

On the daily chart, levels like the 50-day and 200-day moving averages are very significant for AMZN. A breakout or rejection from these levels can dictate the trend for days or weeks.

Look for "Heavy" Price Action

AMZN is a thick stock with high institutional ownership. Watch for signs of accumulation (steady buying) or distribution (steady selling) near our signal levels.

Prime Day & Holiday Season Catalysts

Be extra alert during major retail events like Prime Day and the holiday shopping season. These periods can create unique and powerful trading opportunities for the stock.
