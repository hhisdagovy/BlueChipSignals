General

What is Blue Chip Signals?

Blue Chip Signals is a premium service that provides real-time trading signals for blue-chip stocks including SPY, META, AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, and AMZN. We use a proprietary momentum breakout strategy based on VWAP and MFI indicators to identify high-probability trading opportunities.

How does Blue Chip Signals work?

Our algorithms monitor price action, volume, and momentum indicators throughout the trading day. When a high-probability setup is detected, a signal is immediately sent to your Telegram channel with all the details you need to act on it.

How are the signals delivered?

All signals are delivered instantly via dedicated Telegram channels. Once you subscribe, you'll receive immediate access to the channels included in your plan. Telegram is free to download on iOS, Android, and desktop.

Signals

How many signals can I expect per day?

Each of our signal channels generates between 4 to 7 high-quality signals per trading day, ensuring you have a steady stream of opportunities during live market hours.

What information do the signals include?

Each signal includes the stock ticker, whether it's a call or put, the suggested strike price, the option premium, and the expiration date. You get everything you need to place the trade immediately.

Which stocks and tickers are covered?

We currently cover SPY, META, AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, and AMZN - some of the most liquid and actively traded securities in the market. We are continuously evaluating additional tickers to add to the platform.

Getting Started

Do I need any trading experience?

No prior experience is required. Our signals are designed to be easy to follow for everyone, from complete beginners to seasoned traders. We include all the details you need - entry point, strike price, expiration, and more.

What do I need to get started?

You'll need a brokerage account that supports options trading (such as TD Ameritrade, Webull, or Robinhood), a Telegram account to receive signals, and an active Blue Chip Signals subscription.

How quickly can I access signals after subscribing?

Access is granted within minutes of your subscription being confirmed. You'll receive onboarding instructions with links to your Telegram channels immediately after payment.
