Frequently Asked Questions

What is BlueChip Signals?

BlueChip Signals is a premium service that provides real-time trading signals for blue-chip stocks like SPY, META, AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, and AMZN. We use a proprietary momentum breakout strategy based on VWAP and MFI indicators to identify high-probability trading opportunities.

How are the signals delivered?

All signals are sent instantly via dedicated Telegram channels. Once you subscribe, you'll get access to the channels for the bots included in your plan.

How many signals can I expect per day?

Each of our trading bots generates between 4 to 7 high-quality signals per trading day, ensuring you have a steady stream of opportunities.

Do I need any trading experience?

No, our signals are designed to be easy to follow for everyone, from beginners to experienced traders. We provide all the necessary details, including the exact entry point and suggested options contract.

What do the signals include?

Each signal includes the stock ticker, whether it's a buy (call) or sell (put) signal, the strike price for the suggested options contract, the premium, and the expiration date.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You will retain access to the service for the remainder of your billing period.
