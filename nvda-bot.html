Best Practices for Success

Wait for Confirmation

Patience is key. Always wait for the official signal in the Telegram channel before entering a trade. Never jump the gun based on market movement alone.

Respect the VWAP

Our strategy is heavily based on the Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP). A signal is strongest when the price has a clear breakout above this key level.

Manage Your Risk

Never allocate your entire portfolio to a single trade. We recommend using 1-5% of your capital per signal to ensure longevity and manage risk effectively.

Understand the MFI

The Money Flow Index (MFI) helps us gauge buying pressure. A high MFI on our signals indicates strong momentum, increasing the probability of a successful trade.

Trade Reversal Bounces

In addition to breakouts, our bot identifies reversal bounces off key support levels. These signals offer a chance to enter at the start of a potential trend change.
