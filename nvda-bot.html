Best Practices for Success

Follow the AI Sector News

NVDA is the leader in AI chips. Any major news about AI advancements, new partnerships, or competitor products can cause explosive moves in the stock.

Monitor the Semiconductor Industry (SMH ETF)

The performance of the broader semiconductor sector can provide a strong tailwind or headwind for NVDA. Keep an eye on the SMH ETF as a general industry gauge.

Trade with the Dominant Trend

NVDA is a strong trending stock. Our bot is designed to identify entry points in the direction of the primary trend, not to fight it. Don't try to short a rocket ship.

Look for High Volume Breakouts

A key confirmation for our signals is a surge in trading volume as the price breaks a key level. This indicates strong institutional interest and conviction in the move.

Be Prepared for Parabolic Moves

When NVDA gets going, it can move much further and faster than many other stocks. It's important to have a plan for taking profits into strength during these parabolic runs.
