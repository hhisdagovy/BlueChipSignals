Strategy Snapshot

  • Focus: Key Level Reversals
  • Indicators: RSI & Support/Resistance
  • Alerts: 3-5 Daily
  • Contracts: Weekly, 2-5% OTM

Best Practices for Success

Monitor AWS News

Amazon Web Services is a massive driver of AMZN's profitability. News related to new contracts, services, or competitor performance can significantly impact the stock.

Track E-commerce Trends

Pay attention to retail sales data and consumer spending habits. AMZN's performance during key shopping seasons (like Prime Day or Black Friday) is a major catalyst.

Understand the 'Mean Reversion' Strategy

Unlike momentum stocks, AMZN often pulls back to key technical levels before continuing its trend. Our bot is designed to identify these high-probability reversal points.

Set Price Alerts at Previous Highs/Lows

AMZN respects technical levels. Setting alerts at significant daily or weekly highs and lows can prepare you for a potential entry when the bot signals a reversal.

Look for RSI Divergence

A key confirmation for our signals is RSI divergence, where the stock price makes a new high or low, but the RSI indicator does not. This often precedes a reversal.
