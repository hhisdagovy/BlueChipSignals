META

Meta Platforms, Inc.

Trade the social media giant navigating the future of digital connection. The META bot is designed to trade high-volatility news and earnings events.

Strategy Snapshot

  • Focus: Event-Driven Swings
  • Indicators: Volume Profile & MACD
  • Alerts: 2-4 Daily
  • Contracts: Weekly & Bi-Weekly
  • Method: Gap & Go / Gap Fill

Best Practices for Success

Monitor Ad Revenue & User Growth

META's core business is advertising. Pay close attention to reports on digital ad spending, user engagement metrics, and any news related to its "Family of Apps" (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp).
Ad Revenue Tracker

Trade Around Earnings Reports

META is known for large price swings after its quarterly earnings reports. Our bot is specifically designed to capture the powerful "Gap & Go" or "Gap Fill" moves that often follow.
Earnings Trading Guide

Be Aware of Regulatory News

As a global social media platform, META is constantly under regulatory scrutiny regarding data privacy, antitrust, and content moderation. Headlines on these topics can cause sudden, sharp moves.
Regulatory Guide

Watch Key Price Levels Post-Gap

After an earnings gap, the pre-market high/low and the previous day's high/low become critical support and resistance levels. The bot uses these to time its entries.
Key Levels Guide

Use Tighter Stops on High-Volatility Days

Event-driven moves can be chaotic. It's often wise to use smaller position sizes and tighter stop-losses than you would on a normal trading day to manage the increased risk.
