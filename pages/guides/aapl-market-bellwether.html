Risk Management for Bellwether Trades

Protect your capital when trading index movements based on AAPL signals:

Position Sizing

Limit bellwether trades to 3-5% of portfolio due to correlation risk. Larger positions increase exposure to systematic moves that can affect both AAPL and index ETFs simultaneously.

Time Decay

AAPL leadership effects fade after 2-4 hours in normal conditions. Intraday correlation is strongest in the first 30-60 minutes after major AAPL moves. Plan exits accordingly.

Divergence Risk

Market can decouple from AAPL during major news or Fed events. Always check broader market catalysts before assuming AAPL will drive index direction. Set tight stops when macro headlines dominate.

Volatility Spikes

Correlation breaks down during VIX >25 periods when fear-driven selling dominates. During high volatility, reduce position sizes by 50% and tighten stops to 1% or less to protect capital.

When AAPL Leadership Breaks Down

Fed Announcements

Market follows bond yields instead of AAPL during FOMC meetings

High VIX (>25)

Correlations weaken during extreme volatility and fear-driven selling

Sector Rotation

Energy or defensive sectors can lead during commodity spikes or risk-off moves

Macro Events

Geopolitical crises or banking system stress override individual stock leadership
