Why Retail Trends Move AMZN

GMV & Mix

Electronics, devices, and own-brand mix shift margins and sentiment.

Ads & Attach

Higher ad load and subscription attach (Prime/Video) expand profit narrative.

Logistics

Faster delivery promises and FC efficiency signal cost leverage.

Competitor Pricing

WMT/TGT discounting or BNPL spikes can shift share and margins.

Q2 (Prime Lead-Up)

Prime date chatter, teaser promos, search trends rising.

Q3 (Prime/Back-to-School)

Prime results + BTS demand; strong read-through to Q4.

Q4 (Holiday/Cyber)

Biggest volume; promo depth vs peers critical for margins.
