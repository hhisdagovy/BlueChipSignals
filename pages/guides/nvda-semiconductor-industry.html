Why SMH ETF Matters for NVDA Trading

The VanEck Semiconductor ETF (SMH) represents the broader semiconductor sector performance and serves as a crucial leading indicator for NVDA movements. Understanding sector-wide trends helps predict whether NVDA will outperform or underperform the market.

The Sector Correlation Advantage

NVDA and SMH maintain an 85%+ correlation during normal market conditions. When this correlation breaks down, it often signals either NVDA-specific catalysts or broader sector rotation, creating high-probability trading opportunities.
