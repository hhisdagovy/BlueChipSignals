High-Impact Releases

The S&P 500 moves aggressively on macro data. Know the schedule and understand the mechanics:

  • CPI & Core CPI: Inflation beats or misses reshape Federal Reserve expectations and shift equity risk premium. Watch for 0.3%+ surprises.
  • PPI (Producer Price Index): Upstream price pressure often foreshadows future CPI moves. Pay attention to revisions.
  • FOMC Rate Decision & Powell Press Conference: The dot plot, statement language, and Q&A can re-price terminal rate odds instantly.
  • NFP & Unemployment Rate: Labor market surprises swing growth outlook and yield curve dynamics—indices follow immediately.
  • GDP Reports: Quarterly growth revisions impact forward earnings estimates and market sentiment.

Pre-Release Trading Framework

Plan your trades before the data drops:

  • Check Implied Move: Review weekly SPY straddle pricing to gauge expected volatility range.
  • Reduce Position Size: Cut exposure by 50-70% ahead of binary events—liquidity can vanish during extreme moves.
  • First Reaction vs. Real Move: Fade knee-jerk spikes that stall at key VWAP or previous day's levels.
  • Volatility Crush Strategy: Post-release implied volatility collapses—consider taking profits quickly or selling premium if direction aligns.
  • Wait for Confirmation: Let the initial 15-minute candle close before entering—avoid getting whipsawed by algorithmic reactions.

Pro Trading Tips

  • Set Price Alerts: Place alerts at key technical levels BEFORE data releases so you're ready to act.
  • Know the Consensus: Market moves on surprises, not the absolute number. A "good" CPI that misses expectations can still sell off.
  • Watch the Bond Market: 10-year Treasury yields often lead SPY movement after economic data—monitor TLT for clues.
  • Track Revisions: Previous month's data revisions can be just as impactful as the headline number.
  • Fed Speak Matters: Powell's commentary and voting member speeches can override data—stay plugged into central bank communications.
