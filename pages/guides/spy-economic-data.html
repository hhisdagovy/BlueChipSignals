High-Impact Releases That Move the Tape

The S&P 500 rips or dips on macro prints. Know the schedule, understand the mechanics.

  • CPI & Core CPI: Inflation beats/misses reshape Fed expectations and shift equity risk-premium.
  • PPI: Upstream price pressure often foreshadows future CPI moves—watch revisions too.
  • FOMC Rate Decision & Powell Presser: The dots, statement language, and Q&A can re-price terminal rate odds in seconds.
  • NFP & Unemployment Rate: Labor surprises swing growth outlook and yield curve—indices follow.

Pre-Print Trading Framework

Plan the trade before the number drops:

  • Implied Move: Check weekly SPY straddle pricing to gauge expected range.
  • Position Sizing: Cut size by 50-70% ahead of binary events; liquidity can vanish.
  • First Reaction vs. Real Move: Fade knee-jerk spikes that stall at key VWAP/AVWAP levels.
  • Vol Crush Exit: Post-release IV collapses—harvest theta by selling premium if direction aligns.

  • Bloomberg ECON GP: Professional-grade release tracker with surprise indices.
