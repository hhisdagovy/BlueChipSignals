Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 22, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BlueChip Signals ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our websites and services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by law.

8. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of non‑essential communications.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions other than your own. By using our services, you consent to such processing.
