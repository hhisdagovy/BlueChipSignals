Best Practices for Success

Monitor Key Economic Events

Pay close attention to FOMC meetings, CPI reports, and employment data. The SPY is highly sensitive to macroeconomic news, which can drive significant intraday trends.

Watch for Sector Rotation

Since SPY is an ETF, its movement is a composite of 500 stocks. Watch which sectors (e.g., tech, financials) are leading or lagging to anticipate SPY's next move.

Respect Broad Market Hours

SPY often sees significant volume and movement during the first and last hour of the trading day. Be prepared for key breakouts or reversals during these times.

Use Index-Specific Levels

Track key levels on related indices like /ES (S&P 500 futures) and the VIX (volatility index). A rising VIX often signals fear and can precede a drop in SPY.

Trade With the Trend

SPY often establishes a clear intraday trend. Our signals are most effective when they align with the broader market direction for the day. Avoid fighting a strong trend.
